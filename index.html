Projects

xspense

A Django powered web application that aids in managing daily finances. Organized in a budget-expense format. Users can log in and track expenses and will be notified if they have gone over budget.

Boulder Blast

Blast through bullet-shooting robots and other obstacles to collect all the gems in this multi-level C++ game. Push boulders around to fill up giant ditches so you can cross. Pick up health and ammo goodies to replenish your supplies. Have fun.

Find Me Bubble Tea

Craving for some boba? Use this iOS app to find the 200 closest bubble tea store nearby your current location. Functional world-wide. Store menus, phone numbers, and directions to reach there are fully integrated in this app.

bounce

Rebound falling bubbles before they get sucked off-screen! Apply the universal color theme you prefer from the generated choices to style this iOS application.

MazeRunner

Imagine you are in the movie The Maze Runner. Terrifying Grievers are after you in the Maze. Enter the Glade's story as you take a run to escape Grievers while also tracking your route with this exercise and health iOS app. Be notified when you win badges after certain distances and reach milestones in the story plotline.
